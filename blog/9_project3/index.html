My Role

My role for the speculative communication network project called Nova, is a combination of some asset illustration and css styling and animation. The goal I have in mind is to ensure that the assets are interactive and as immersive as possible to pull the users in and keep them engaged. I plan to elaborate extensively this idea that the futuristic outlook on communication channels will transcend the human species by connecting every living organisms to one another, using this application as a way to bridge the gap between their communication limitations. To do this, I am grabbing inspiration from Avatar (the blue people) with the willow/wisteria style tree that breathes life into every organisim that it connects with. I plan to make the visuals very animated and technologically focused, as if we have abused the boundaries that the world has to offer. AKA this tree will have a lot of optic fiber inspired wires and werid nicknacks to make it seem out of this current world.
